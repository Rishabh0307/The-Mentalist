PATRICK JANE

Patrick Jane is the primary protagonist. He is a former consultant to the California Bureau of Investigation. He uses his keen powers of observation, his brilliant mind and his talent for mischief to solve crimes. His motivation for joining CBI is to find and kill Red John.

TERESA LISBON

She is one of the main protagonists.Lisbon is a former senior agent and leader of the "Serious Crimes Unit" for the California Bureau of Investigation (CBI). After leaving the CBI, she served as a sheriff for a small town in Washington state, but left that position to join the Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI).

RED JOHN

Red John is the alias of the central antagonist in The Mentalist during its first six seasons. While he was alive, Red John was California's most notorious serial killer. He was also the founder of the Blake Association, a secret criminal organization of law enforcemet officials that committed and covered up his crimes.
